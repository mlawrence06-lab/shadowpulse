# ShadowPulse

**Transform your Bitcointalk.org experience with real-time community sentiment, enhanced discovery tools, and modern UI upgrades.**

ShadowPulse is the ultimate companion for the **Bitcointalk.org** forum, designed to modernize the way you interact with the community. It introduces a powerful layer of engagement, allowing you to see what the community truly values through a unique voting and ranking system, all while providing essential utilities like advanced search and live market data.

## Features

### Community Voting & Sentiment
*   **Voice Your Opinion:** Upvote or downvote topics and posts to help surface high-quality content.
*   **ShadowPulse Score:** Instantly see the community's trust and engagement level for every user and topic via the ShadowPulse score (SP).
*   **Dynamic Ranking:** Users are ranked based on their contributions and community reception, giving you immediate context on who you are interacting with.

### Advanced Reports & Discovery
*   **Top Charts:** Discover the highest-rated topics, most active discussions, and top-performing members.
*   **Trending Content:** Never miss a beat with reports on the most visited boards and active threads.
*   **Bitlist Search:** Integrated directly into your toolbar, the Bitlist engine provides superior search capabilities to find exactly what you're looking for within the forum.

### Modern Toolbar Interface
*   **Sleek Design:** A non-intrusive, floating toolbar at the bottom of your screen gives you access to all tools without cluttering the page.
*   **Real-Time Data:** View the current Bitcoin price and market trends directly in the toolbar.
*   **Minimizable:** Need screen space? The smart toolbar auto-hides or minimizes based on your preference.

### Privacy & Security
*   **Secure:** Built with security in mind, ensuring your forum interactions remain safe.
*   **Transparent:** We respect your data. The extension uses local storage to save the following specific data items: `theme` (UI appearance setting), `toolbarLeft` (Toolbar X-coordinate), `autoMin` (Toolbar minimize preference), `minimized_show_price` (Price visibility in minimized mode), `searchEngine` (Search provider selection), `memberUuid` (Anonymous installation token), `memberId` (Anonymous user index), and `memberRestoreAck` (Restoration state flag). No personal user data is permanently stored or sold.

**Bitcointalk.org** is the legendary home of cryptocurrency discussion. ShadowPulse honors that legacy by bringing modern features to the classic interface, empowering the community to self-regulate and highlight the best content.
